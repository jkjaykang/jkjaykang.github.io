Oyster Chec's Compedium

"It's gonna take me ages to write about all the creatures I have encountered"

-Oyster Chec, Halfling Warlock

The Hemiad Explosion changed every aspect of the world, including the creatures and plants inhabiting the world. While many explorers and scientists study the world and its inhabitants, there is still a countless number of them to be discoverd. So the next time you explore the dark caves and the tallest mountains, keep your eyes peeled, because you might come across a creature you have never seen before.

Although the world is brimming with friendly and docile creatures, dangers lurk as well. Creatures that disobey the natural order of the world, and pose danger to people are commonly referred to as monsters. Monster often resemble a twisted and demented version of existing creatures, and sometimes even take a shape akin to that of a human. Most monsters are active and hunting at night, so try not to roam around at night, unless you are a hunter or an explorer brave enough to hunt down a monster in its den.
